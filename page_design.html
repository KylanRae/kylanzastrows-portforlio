Page Design

This page is where my page layouts are displayed. Most of the layouts are from my time in many of my classes. This category is my favourite part of the design process.

GRPH 148 | Digital Page Layout 2 | 5.2.3 Ads Project

This design is a 2/3-page ad for a magazine. The required elements were; two or more images, which must fill the ad dimensions, Merida Bikes logo not larger than 1" in width and the supplied text. We had to layout the page based on a magazine specification base sent by the "magazine company." It needed to be at 300ppi.

GRPH 148 | Digital Page Layout 2 | 5.2.2 Owera Vineyards Ad

This design is a half-page ad for a magazine. The required elements were; one or more images, which must fill the ad dimensions, Owera Vineyards logo not larger than 1" in width and the supplied text. We had to layout the page based on a magazine specification base sent by the "magazine company." It needed to be at 300ppi.

GRPH 148 | Digital Page Layout 2 | 5.2.1 Club Med Ad

This design is a full-page ad for a magazine. The required elements were; one or more images that must fill the ad dimensions, the Club Med logo that could not be larger than 1" in width and the club's tagline. It was to promote their vacations. We had to layout the page based on a magazine specification base sent by the "magazine company." It needed to be at 300ppi.

GRPH 148 | Digital Page Layout 2 | 4.2.3 Brochure Project

This brochure was one of the projects for marks we did after the practice. The requirements were to use; all of the supplied text and at least one of the supplied logos. We were allowed to find our images on royalty-free image sites. The unfolded image was 14"x8.5" at 300ppi as a Gate Fold.

GRPH 148 | Digital Page Layout 2 | 4.2.1 Tyte Lids Brochure

This brochure was our first-page layout for the class. The requirements were to use; the supplied text, at least two of the supplied images and use both of the supplied logos. We received lots of feedback from our teacher and was encouraged to do so for success. The unfolded brochure was 11"x8.5" at 300ppi and folded as either a Z-fold or a Tri-fold. I chose to do the Tri-fold setup.

GRPH 150 | Design 1 | Performance Exam (Printed Piece Portion)

The postcard above was for my design 1 class during the second term of my first semester in my first year in Graphic Communications. Our performance exam consisted of making a logo for one of the four prompts of our choosing. I chose the recording studio option. For that option, we have complete creative freedom, but we were only allowed to design in greyscale, to reduce costs. This piece was the advertising piece that would be left in music stores across Canada to promote their new recording studio.

GRPH 147 | Digital Page Layout 1 | Performance Test: Newsletter

The newsletter above was my first attempt at doing any creative page layout for my first-page layout assignment. This specific assignment was during the first term of my first semester in my first year of Graphic Communications. Our task was to design a newsletter talking about a particular day in history, our birthday. We had to research things that happened that day, and the year we were born. The specifications were that we have two pages, a minimum of 2 columns on each page and must have two master pages, an A-Master and a B-Master.
