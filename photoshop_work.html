Photoshop Work

This page is where my photoshop compositions are displayed. These are all designed during my photoshop class or whenever I have decided a composite was needed.

GRPH 155 | Image Editing 2 | Use a Series of Supplied Photographs to Design Composition Music Album Covers Assignment

This image is a grunge album cover, designed for a vinyl cover. Our requirements to use at least one photo from each of the categories supplied to use, from supplied images: a person, an instrument and a texture. The vinyl cover was required to be 12.375"x12.375" at 300ppi.

GRPH 155 | Image Editing 2 | Use Customized Raster Tools to Create a Postcard Assignment

This image is a postcard that we had a choice of where or what it was for. I chose to make it for Alaska and make the northern lights. We were required to use two custom photoshop brushes, two custom shapes, and a custom text. The postcard specifications were to be 5.8"x4.1" at 300ppi.

GRPH 155 | Image Editing 2 | Design a Movie Poster in Photoshop Assignment

This image is a horror movie poster from my Movie Poster assignment. We needed to use at least one blend mode, fill layer and layer effect. The poster needed to be 8.5"x11" at 300ppi.

GRPH 155 | Image Editing 2 | Use Shape Tools and Custom Shape Tools to Create a Greeting Card Assignment

This image is a valentine's day card I designed for this assignment. We needed to use two custom shapes in photoshop and one shape using the photoshop tools. The size of the cover of the card was 4.25"x5.5" at 300ppi.

GRPH 155 | Image Editing 2 | Creative Transformations Assignment

These composites are from the first assignment from my Image Editing 2 class. We had to add either text or an image we had found from royalty-free image websites to 5 of the supplied images using photoshop. The images needed to be either 8"x10" or 10"x8" at 300ppi.

GRPH 153 | Image Editing 1 | Performance Assessment 2

These images were a part of a graded assignment that we had to do in my Image Editing 1 class that happened during my first term of my first semester of my first year in Graphic Communications. For this assignment, we had to create two compositions that included non-destructive editing techniques. Each image had to have three or more objects in the final composite image. The photos had to be designed as 8 inches x 10 inches or 10 inches x 8 inches at 300ppi.

GRPH 153 | Image Editing 1 | Performance Assessment 1

This image was a part of a graded assignment that I we had to do in my Image Editing 1 class that happened during my first term of my first semester of my first year in Graphic Communications. For this assignment, we had four images that needed us to edited in photoshop to specifications, and we got the change to create our composition. We had to combine two or more images into one final composite image. It had to be 4 inches x 6 inches at 300ppi. Layer masks were required as well.
